////// Our Mission

"A closet full of clothes — and somehow nothing to wear."

We're Cheeky-Fit Inc., a Delaware C-Corp building the social network and marketplace where people share their clothing choices with the world and retailers recommend their offering directly to consumers — powered by AI.

We're not building another shopping app or another social feed. We're creating a new category: where identity, trust, and commerce meet. Fashion is social by nature. Cheeky makes it shoppable.

////// Business Model
Transaction Fees

Revenue generated when users purchase retailer recommendations — not from selling your attention to advertisers.

Promoted Placements

Retailers pay for precision placement in front of users who actually match their product — powered by real wardrobe data.

Demand Insights

Aggregated, anonymized wardrobe trends that help brands reduce overproduction and plan inventory with precision.
